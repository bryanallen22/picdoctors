Pic Doctor Frequently Asked Questions

Who can become a Pic Doctor?

Due to restrictions from our payments provider, you must be a US resident with a bank account to become a Pic Doctor. We hope that this changes in the coming months!

How does it work?

We've got a full page to answer that question here.

How am I protected as a Doctor?

As a doctor we protect you by watermarking your images until a user has accepted your work as valid and complete. Likewise we've put a hold on the user's account to verify that they can pay for the work.

What is a merchant account, and why do I need it?

For an in depth look at merchant accounts view the wikipedia page here. Essentially a merchant account allows you to accept payment from a user. This is how you as a doctor will receive your payments. It's fully integrated into our site so you don't have to navigate between our site and our payment processing partner.

Who can see my work?

Right now, the job can only be seen if the user allows sharing.

What happens if the user isn't happy with my work?

We encourage users to request modifications to your work, but they have the option of returning the job to the market. If this happens, you won't be paid. We hope it doesn't happen often!

Can I communicate with the user?

Yep. Just use the 'comments' below the pictures, and we'll notify them.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

Just visit the withdraw page and have the money transferred to your account. The minimum amount that you can withdraw at a time is {{ minimum_withdraw | currency_dollars }}.

How am I paid as a Doctor?

Doctor's are given a base pay of 50% with a possible maximum of 70%. Your possible payout is calculated when you take a job. The calculated value depends on the amount of accepted pictures you've had in the last 30 days and your overall rating as a doctor(Tier 2 and above). When the user accepts your work the possible payout is then credited to your merchant account.
Payment Scenario (Tier 2 + 5 Star rating):
  • User lists a simple job worth $2 on the market.
  • Your take: $ 1.2
  • Bank Fees: $ 0.36
  • PicDoctor Fees: $ 0.44
Payment Scenario (Tier 4 + 5 Star rating):
  • User lists a simple job worth $2 on the market.
  • Your take: $ 1.4
  • Bank Fees: $ 0.36
  • PicDoctor Fees: $ 0.24

Job Statuses

Available in Market

The job has been placed in the marketplace for doctor's to view and accept.

Job not taken, consider upping price

The job has been sitting in the market too long and no doctor has accepted the job. The job price may be too low, consider upping the price.

Doctor Accepted Job

The job has been placed in the marketplace for doctor's to view and take.

Doctor Submitted

The doctor has submitted work awaiting the User to accept their work.

Work Submitted, Approval Pending

The Doctor has submitted work, but since the Doctor is new to Pic Doctors their work must be approved by a moderator first.

User Accepted Work

The user has accepted the Doctor's Work. At this point the hold on the user's credit card is charged and the doctor's account is credited.

Job Refunded

The user has chosen to get a refund and not charge their credit card. Note after the user has viewed the doctor's work and accepted the job, they can no longer request a refund.
