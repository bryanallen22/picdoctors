1. Upload your pictures

Upload a single picture or a full album by dragging and dropping the files into place. If you'd like two or more pictures combined into a single picture, group them together.

 

2. Describe the job

Give the Pic Doctor a description of what you'd like done to your photos. You can highlight particular areas with their own descriptions.

3. Set a price

You choose how much you are willing to pay for the job. We'll place a temporary hold on your card for your chosen amount, but we won't charge it yet. By offering more, you're more likely to get an experienced, trusted Pic Doctor. If nobody is willing to do the work for the amount you offer, we'll let you know.

 

4. A Pic Doctor accepts the job

Once someone accepts your job, we'll pull it from the public market while they work on it.

5. Communicate

Your Pic Doctor may have further questions or clarifications about the work that you would like done. We'll give you the chance to communicate, while keeping your email addresses confidential.

 

6. Approve the work

Once the Pic Doctor finishes, you'll get a chance to approve a watermarked version of the work. If you want further modifications, you can request them. If you aren't satisfied, you can put the job back on the public market or request a refund. You won't be charged unless you are satisfied.

7. Enjoy your pictures

Once you've approved the images, you'll receive the full resolution doctored photos. They are totally yours now - you own the full rights to you them in any way that you wish.
