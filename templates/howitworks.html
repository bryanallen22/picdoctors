1. Upload your pictures

Upload a single picture or a series of pictures by dragging and dropping the files into place. If you'd like two or more pictures combined into a single picture, group them together.

 

2. Describe the job

Give the Pic Doctor a general description of what you'd like changed in your photos. Then select specific areas of your pictures that require more detailed instructions.

3. Set a price

Choose how much you are willing to pay for the job. We'll place a temporary hold on your card for the chosen amount, but we won't charge it yet. By offering more, you're more likely to get an experienced, trusted Pic Doctor. If your job isn't taken at the price you offer, we'll let you know.

 

4. Job is accepted

When your doctor accepts the job, they'll start working on it, and it won't be visible to other Pic Doctors.

5. Communicate

Your Pic Doctor may have further questions or clarifications about the work that you would like done. We give you the chance to communicate, while keeping your email address confidential.

 

6. Approve the work

Once the Pic Doctor completes the work, you can approve a watermarked version. If you want further modifications, you can request them. If you aren't satisfied, you can put the job back on the public market or request a refund. You won't be charged unless you are satisfied.

7. Enjoy your pictures

Once you've approved the changes, you'll receive the full-resolution, doctored photos. You own full rights to these pictures—you can use them in any way that you wish.
